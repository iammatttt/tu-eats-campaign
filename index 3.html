TU Eats: Expand OneCard Freedom

Advocating for meal plan access beyond campus

Why Expand the TU OneCard?

At Towson University, our meal plan is limited to on-campus dining and Grubhub delivery. But imagine if we could grab a smoothie at Tropical Café or eat dinner with friends at Subway off York Road—using our OneCard.

How Does Towson Compare?

School Meal Plan Cost Off-Campus Use? Notes
Towson University $1,300–$3,100 ❌ No Grubhub free delivery only
Stevenson University $2,500–$3,100 ✅ Yes Use Flex Dollars at CVS, Dunkin’, Qdoba, etc.
University of Maryland $2,500–$3,200 ✅ Limited Some campus franchise use allowed
